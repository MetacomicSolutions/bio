How to Install the App

Android

Open Chrome and go to the PWA website.
Tap the three dots menu in the top-right corner.
Select "Add to Home screen" and follow the prompts.
The app will appear on your home screen, ready to use!

Desktop

Open Chrome or Edge and visit the PWA website.
Click the "Install" button in the address bar or select the three dots menu and choose "Install [App Name]".
Follow the prompts to add the app to your system.
The app will be installed and can be accessed from your desktop!

iPhone

Open Safari and go to the PWA website.
Tap the share icon (the square with an upward arrow).
Select "Add to Home Screen".
The app will be added to your home screen and is ready to use!
